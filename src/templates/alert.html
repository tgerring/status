2015-07-17 Go client 0.9.36 Denial-of-Service attack

Is my Ether safe?

Yes. This issue did not compromise the integrity of the blockchain, only availability of nodes.

What happened?

A denial-of-service attack was discovered in the Go client, enabling a malicious miner to mine a block that would crash any Go client attemping to process it. The Go client would panic due to not properly handling errors returned by the EC recover operation that is executed on every tx in a block.

What is being done

This issue is fixed in version 0.9.38 of the Go client.
